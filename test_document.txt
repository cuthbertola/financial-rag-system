Apple Inc. Q4 2024 Financial Results

Revenue: $89.5 billion, up 6% year-over-year
iPhone Revenue: $43.8 billion
Services Revenue: $22.3 billion, a new record
Net Income: $22.9 billion
Earnings per share: $1.47
Operating margin: 29.8%
The company returned $27 billion to shareholders through dividends and share buybacks.
